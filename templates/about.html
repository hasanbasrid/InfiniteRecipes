What is Infinite Recipes?

Infinite Recipes is a social media approach about food combined with grocery shopping. It allows you to post recipes, comment on and rate recipes, and easily buy ingredients by getting redirected to a shopping website. Infinite Recipes is a school project for CS 490 (Design in Software Engineering).
